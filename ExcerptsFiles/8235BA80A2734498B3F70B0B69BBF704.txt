Acute Gastric Infections
HYPERTROPHIC GASTROPATHY
INC IN GASTRIC RUGAL FOLDS
- cerebroform appearance
1. menetrier disease
Sym: diarrhea, peripheral edema.
- gross enlarged rugal folds
- dec acid production
- protein loosing enteropathy
- gastric enteropathy
- inc risk of cancer gastric
- LM: profound hyperplasia of mucous cells with glandulary atrophy


2. ZE syndrome
- mass in pancreae
- Inc secretion of gastrin
- inc secretion of acid
- inc ulcers, hyperacidity
- MULTIPLE INTRACTABLE PEPTIC ULCERS.
- in p.gastrin, mass in pancreas

3. Stress ulcers
- burns, trauma
- superficial: mucosa and submucosa involved, treatable.
- sec to burns, trauma: CURLING ULCERS
- INC ICP: CUSHING ULCERS

4. Acute Hemorrhagic gastritis
- KEY: ALCOHOL, NSAID, ASPIRIN
- NEUTROPHIL RELEASE ENZYMES CAUSING HEMORRHAGE
- UPPER GI BLEEDING
