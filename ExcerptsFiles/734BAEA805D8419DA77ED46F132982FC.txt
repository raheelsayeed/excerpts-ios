NEPHRITIC Syndrome
- Glomerular Cell Profliferation (either in glomerulus or via inflammation, Compression of a capillaries, decrease in blood flow)
- Hematuria by red blood cell casts (tubular shape), due to wall damage.
- infl of glomerulus (mild to moderate proteinuria) (NO PITTING EDEMA)
- Inflamming of Glomerulus GFR dec, less active Nephrons.
- Oliguria
- builds up Na - Hypertension (hypernatremia).
- Nephrons are damaged, due to inflammation.
