Hyaline arteriosclerosis / RDS
- collapsed alveoli.
- massive atelectasis (collapsed airways)
- deficient surfactant. (decreases surface tension)
- Collapsing pressure: 
Causes:
1. Prematurity (not enough surfactant) (give mammy glucocorticoids that stimulate surfactant production), thyroid hormone, prolactin.
2. Gestational Diabetes.
3. C-section. Stress is missing. ACTH and cortisol are not increased, no surfactant production.
