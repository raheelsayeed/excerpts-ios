Procainamide
Class IA antiArrhythmic drug. Blocks active or open state fast sodium channels.
Action:
- M receptor blockade, (less when compared to Quinidine)
- metabolized by NAcetylTransferase

Adverse
- good Hapten (binds to tissue proteins and triggers immune response causing drug induced SLE.
- hematoxicity
- CV effects : torsades

Lab:
- pos antiHistone antibody (drug induced SLE)
- positive ANA
- neg dsDNA
- neg AntiSM
