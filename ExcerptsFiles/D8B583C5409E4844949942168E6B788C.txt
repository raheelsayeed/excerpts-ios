Polyarteritis Nodosa
Aka Kussmaul disease
Every organ affected except lungs
- small blood vessels affected.
- associated usually with hepatitis B infection

Clinical:
- depending on the organ involved
- low grade fever
- abdominal pain
- hematuria
- renal failure
- Arthralgia



- type 3 hypersensitivity
- thrombosis, infarction
- segmental necrotising vasculitis.
- fibrinoid necrosis.



Male, IV drug abuser, pANCA or cANCA
