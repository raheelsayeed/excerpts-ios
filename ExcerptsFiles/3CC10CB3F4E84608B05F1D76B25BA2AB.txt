Post Strep Glomerulonephritis
- Post strep beta-hem groupA, cross reaction with M-protein results in deposition of immune complexes on glomerulus. 
- sub epithelial deposits (from which podocytes arise)
- Nephritic
- [lab] AS-O titre, HYALURONIDASE, low Serum Complement
- [LM] Hypercellular gl, Neutro,monocytes, redcell casts
- [IMF] granular deposits of IgG,IgM C3.
- [EM] Subepithelial Humps granular pattern
