Deep Vein Thrombosis
Hx of stasis (bedridden, postman, farmers)
Hex of injury, damage to intima
Hyper coagulate state secondary to cancers

Unilateral leg swelling, erythema, pain.
Often asymtomatic
- deep veins are iliac, femoral, popliteal.



Dx
Doppler studies

Complications
Embolus (pulmonary)
Sudden death
Small emboli can lead to innards

Rx
Walk around
Stalkings compress the superficial veins increasing flow into the deep veins
Heparin
