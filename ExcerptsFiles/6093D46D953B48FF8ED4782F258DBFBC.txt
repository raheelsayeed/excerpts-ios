Angiotensin Converting Enzyme Inhibitors
blocks angiotensionI to angiotension II.
Results in reduced stimulation of AT-1 receptor

Two consequential actions:
1. Reduced ALD secretion, decreased retention, reduction in preload
2. BV dilation.
