Atrial Natriuretic Peptide
- hormone from atria
- stimulated by the stretch of atria (congestive heart failure)
- presents with dyspnea, ANP test can be done to differential Pulmonary and Cardiac cause of dyspnea if CXR is inconclusive
- Promotes GFR
- dilation of afferent and Constriction of Efferent.
- Actions: Natriuresis (inhibits reabsorption of Na and water)
