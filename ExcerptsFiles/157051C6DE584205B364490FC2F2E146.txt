Coarctation: postductal
1. By definition this is the adult type, and Ductus arteriosus is closed.
2. Stenosis is blow the subclavian artery
3. High pressure in the upper extremity
4. Low pressure and pulse in the lower extremity.
5. Heart compensated and increases pressure and beats
6. Notching of intercostal arteries seen on XRay. These arteries will erode the ribs.
7. Associated with congestive h f, and intracerebral hemorrhage and rarely dissecting aneurysm.
