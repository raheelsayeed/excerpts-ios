Fibrocartilage
Type I and Type II Collagen 
gives it greater resistance to being stretched (tensile strength)

Site: Intervertebral disks of vertebral column and menisci of the knee. Also may form the attachment of ligaments and tendons to bone.

Composition: Lacks perichondrium and has less water in its extracellular matrix compared to other cartilages.
