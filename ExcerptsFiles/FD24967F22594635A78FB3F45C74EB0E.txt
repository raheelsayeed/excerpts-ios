Quinidine
Class Ia antiArrhythmic
Na Ch block
- blocks Open or Active state, M and H gate open
- dec I.na
- Inc APD
- Inc ERP
- also blocks K+ channel

QUINIDINE
- M receptor blockade
- alpha receptor block (lowers BP)

Use:
1. atrial fibrillation
However, would require digitalization.
Buzz: Chinchonism
Rorsade: GI dysfunction, ocular damage, hypotension.

Torsades: prolongation of qrs complex and QT interval

Adverse: 
Hyper kalemia
Digoxincv
