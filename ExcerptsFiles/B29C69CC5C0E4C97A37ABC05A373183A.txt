Chronic Bronchitis
Clinical diagnosis: persistent cough with sputum for at least 3 months in two consecutive years

S/s
- cough, sputum, dyspnea
- hypoxia, cyanosis, wt gain
- blue bloaters

Histology: hypertrophy and hyperplasia mucous glands
- hypersecretion
- bronchial squamous hyperplasia


Complication:
- recurrent infections
- Secondary P HTN
- arHF
- Lung cancer
