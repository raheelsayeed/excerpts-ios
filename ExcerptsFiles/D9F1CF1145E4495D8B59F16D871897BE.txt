Loop Diuretics
ACUTE PUL EDEMA, CONGESTIVE HEART FAILURE: DRUG OF CHOICE (LOOP) ETHACRYNIC ACID

SIDE EFFECTS
- HYPERSENS
- HYPOKAL , ALKALOSIS (ALD)
- HYPOCAL, HYPOMAG
- HYPERURICEMIA (WEAK ACID) (PREEXISTING GOUT): PUMPS IN PCT secret weak acid,bases (urate secretion), many drugs competitively inhibit urate secretion. most of Antibiotics are weak acids. (Penicillins, cephalosporins)
- OTOTOXIC (LOSS OF HEARING, ETHACRYNIC > FUROSEMIDE) 

DRUG INTERACTIONS CHRONICALLY USED.
- AMINOGLYCOSIDES
- LITHIUM,  DIGOXIN (COMPETES WITH DIGOXIN)
