Nitroprusside
Direct acting vasodilator, acts on both arterioles and veins.
Dec in TPR
Use: emergency HTN

PRUSS: cyanide toxicity, drug should not be taken more than 24-36 hours
Which is why it is administered with nitrites and thiosulfate, thiocyanide is less toxic.
Nitrites form methehemoglobin which has greater affinity for cyanide. Cyanide will thus not interfere wit the electron transport chain.
