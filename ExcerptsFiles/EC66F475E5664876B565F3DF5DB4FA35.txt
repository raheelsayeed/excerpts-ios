Lung COMPLIANCE
COMPLIANCE = ΔVOLUME / ΔPRESSURE
COMPLIANCE ~ ΔVOLUME = 1/~ ELASTICITY.
COMPLIANCE 1/~ ΔPRESSURE.
COMPLIANCE 1/~ ELASTICITY (Elastin: A1AT: deficient in Emphysema)

COMPLIANCE 1/~ RECOIL


ELASTICITY IS THE ABILITY TO RETURN TO ORIGINAL STATE.
INVERSLY PROPORTIONAL TO ΔV

RECOIL: 2 forces
- SURFACE TENSION: When liquid-air interphase, it is a collapsing force. Alveoli lined with a layer of moisture, filled with air, and alveolar wall is soft. Should be collapsing!
- LaPlace Law (PRESSURE INVERSE TO RADIUS)
SHIFT OF AIR FROM SMALL ALVEOLI TO LARGER ALVEOLI, (SMALL COLLAPSES).

While alveoli are collapsed, 2-3 weeks before birth type II Pneumocytes in lung synthesise Surfactant.
