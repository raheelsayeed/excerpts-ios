In acute respiratory disorder, decrease in PCO2 by 10mmHg ~ HCO3- by 2 mMolar

Compensation is only by Kidney but the response is delayed by at least 6 hours

Hence in Acute Changes, immediate response is only Hypo or Hyperventilation (PCo2)
