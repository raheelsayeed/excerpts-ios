PROTEIN MEDIATED Solute Transport
- D-Glucose is the natural isomer of Glucose, not L-Glucose (non natural isomer).
- CHEMICAL STRUCTURE: same structure. compete for same carrier,
eg. glucose and galactose compete for the same carrier protein.

[question] In protein mediated transport, adding galactose would compete with glucose, hence the rate of transport should decrease, if not, it is "Simple Diffusion".
