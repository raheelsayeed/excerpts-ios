RPGN
- ALL NEPHRITIC SYNDROMES CAN LEAD TO RPGN
- aka CRESCENTIC GN
- PROLIFERATION OF PARIETAL CELLS OF BOWMAN'S CAPSULE, compress glomerulus.
- FOLLOWS: GD SYNDR, PSGN, SLE, BERGER DISEASE
- ASSOCIATED: VASCULITIS (WEGENER GRANULOMATOSIS)
- IDIOPATHIC
- [LM] HYPERCELLULAR	, crescent in bowmanspace
- [IMF] Granular or Linear (antiGBM) of Ig & Complement, GBM DISRUPTION.
- POOR PROGNOSIS WITH RAPID PROGRESSION TO ARF, ESRD.
