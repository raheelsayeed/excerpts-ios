Pulmonary SURFACTANT
1. DECREASES SURFACE TENSION FORCE, DECREASES COLLAPSING ABILITY, INCREASES COMPLIANCE.

2. OPPOSES LAPLACE EFFECT ON ALVEOLI, STABILISES SMALL ALVEOLI.

3. DEC CAPILLARY FILTRATION FORCE.

* DEFICIENCY OF SURFACTANT: (LECITHIN:SPHINGOMYELIN RATION <2)

SYMPTOMS OF RDS:
- Inspiratory difficulty
- Increase in Surface tension force, decrease in compliance: ATELECTASIS (GENERALISED COLLAPSE OF ALVEOLI).
- PULMONARY EDEMA (WHEEZES) DEC IN CAPILLARY FILTRATION
