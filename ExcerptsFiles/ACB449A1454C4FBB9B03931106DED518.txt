Lipoprotein lipase activation
- apoC-II
- INSULIN (GENETIC)

Promotes transport of cholesterol into the adipocyte and stores it.

As apoC is given back to HDL, VLDL is now IDL.
IDL (APOE, APOB100) enters liver through a receptor and goes the similarly under lysosomal degradation.

Second fate of IDL: in BLOOD IDL gives CHOL to HDL. This HDL-CHOL.
HDL-CHOL has apoA which esterify CHOL and gives it back to IDL.
