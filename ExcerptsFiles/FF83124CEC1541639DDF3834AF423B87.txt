Cholesterol Metabolism
0. Nothing happens in Mouth, Stomach.

Intestine
1. Chol + CE ---{g.Bile + P.esterase}-->Chol + FA

Intestine Mucosal Cell
2. Chol + FA -{ACAT Enzyme}-> CE + Chylomicron
Chylomicron is a physical complex of TG + CE + PhosphoLipids + Apoprotein B-48.

3. Chylomyicron: into the Blood stream via Lymph (Thoracic duct)



PATH:
Abetalipoproteinemia caused by a defect in Apoprotein-B-48.
Results in 
↓TG Serum
↓Cholesterol Serum


Graph: BloodChol/Time (Postmeal)
- Bell Shaped
- Normal baseline max is 200 mg/dL
- Peaks 4-5 hrs
- Normal after 7 hrs
- Transient Cholesterolemia for 2-7 hrs post meal.
- Similar to Triglycerides Graph.

CONTRAST: Blood Glucose Graph.
- Peak: 45 mins
- Normal: 2-3 hrs
- Because No emulsification or convertion necessary. Glucose is readily absorbed by the intestinal mucosal cells.
- contrast to glucose curve
