Adult Respiratory Distress Syndrome
Damage to capillary endothelial cell. fibrin leaks out and line the alveolar spaces that block gaseous exchange
- Scar tissue is formed.
- Fibrosis results: Restrictive lung disease.

etio
- shock
- sepsis
- trauma
- gastric aspiration
- oxygen toxicity
- drugs
- pulmonary infections


Syn: difuse alveolar damange, shock lung, acute lung injury

clinical: 
- dyspnea
- tachypnea
- hypoxemia.
- cyanosis
- use of accessory respiratory muscles

xray
- bilateral lung opacity ("white lung")

path:
- gross: heavy,
- histology: interstitial and intraalveolar edema, interstitial inflammation, loss of type 1 pneumocytes, hyaline membrane formation.

hyaline membrane formation: cellular debri of dead pneumocytes + fibrin-rich edema

Rx
--
O2, PEEP and mechanical ventilation.

Prognosis: 50%.
