A-a Gradient
If > 30, high specificity for pathology

A-a: 
Hypoxemia due to V/Q defect or outside lungs
- .PaCO2 (respiratory acidosis)

Barbiturates: 0 gradient

If A-a is normal, some pathology outside the lungs
1. Resp center depression
- diaphragm paralysis
- resp airway obstruction
