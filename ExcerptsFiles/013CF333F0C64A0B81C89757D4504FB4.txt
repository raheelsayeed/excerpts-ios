EFFECT OF IPP ON PULMONARY BLOOD FLOW
Pr  InverselyProportional to Volume

INSPIRATION:
- LOW: INTRA THORACIC PRESSURE 
- HIGH: VENOS RETURN TO THE LUNG
- BLOOD TEMPORARILY POOLED IN THE LUNG DURING INSPIRATION.
- LOW: FLOW TO LEFT HEART (LA) MOMENTARILY
- LOW: FLOW TO LEFT VENTR MOMENTARILY
- LOW: CARDIAC OUTPUT MOMENTARILY.
- LOW BLOOD PRESSURE MOMENTARILY.
- BARORECEPTOR REFLEX/RESPONSE INCREASE HR.

THIS IS PARADOXICAL MOVEMENT OF BLOOD PRESSURE.

[V] TAKING BP, MAKE PATIENT CALM. (about 10mmHg difference)
