Free Water Clearance
It is the best measure of balance between solute and water excretion.

Use: determine whether kidneys are responding appropriately to maintain normal plasma osmolarity

It is how much solute free water is excreted

PURE FREE WATER LOST IN URINE

PlasmaOsm Low: + FWClearanc
Result: hypotonic Urine

PlasmaOsm High: - FWClearance
Result hypertonic Urine
