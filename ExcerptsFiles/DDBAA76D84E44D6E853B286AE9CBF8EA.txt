Triglyceride mobilization in starvation state
Adipocytes:
TG --{hormone sensitive lipase}-- FA + Glycerol
- h-sen-lipase is activated by phosphorylation in starvation states by
1. dec Insulin (starvation)
2. Inc Epinephrine (exercise)
3. Inc Cortisol

FA-alb and Glycerol enter liver

Glycerol enters the pathway of Gluconeogenesis
This is upregulated by 
- glucagon
- cortisol

FA in Liver - Beta Oxidation : AcetylCoA

- Beta-Oxydation provides energy for Gluconeogenesis.
- AcetylCoA promotes Gluconeogenesis
- AcetylCoA  used further for
  - ketogenesis
  - Kreb cycle
