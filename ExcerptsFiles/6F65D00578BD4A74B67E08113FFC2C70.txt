Hydralazine
Direct active vasodilator through NitricOxide (NO)
- selective arteriolar dilator.
- reduces TPR
- Use in a moderate to severe hypertension

ProPregnancy: high protein binding, and little free fraction to cross over into the fetus, like methyldopa.

SE:
1. SLE high immunogenicity like procainamide and isoniazide
2. Edema (use diuretics)
3. Reflex tachycardia (coadmin beta blocker)
