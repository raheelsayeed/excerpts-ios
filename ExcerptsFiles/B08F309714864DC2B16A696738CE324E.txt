Infective Endocarditis
Strep veridians most common
Staph aureus 2nd common affects normal or damaged valve

Common: mitral valve, aortic valve
IV drug abuse: tricuspid valve.

Valve vegetation

Colon involvement: ulceration of colon mucosa like colon cancer or ulcerative colitis caused by streptococcus bolvus
