Hypertension drug choices with conditions
1. Angina: BB, CCB
3. PostMI: BB prophylaxis
2. BPH: alpha blocker
4. Dyslipidemia: alpha blocker, CCB, ACEI/ARB (bb and thiazides lead to increase in LDL)
5. Diabetes: ACEI, ARB
6. Heart failure: ACEI, ARB, BB
