Bacterial Pnemonia
- productive cough
- dullness
- richi rales
- .breathsounds
- shift to the left

1. Lobar Pnemonia (entire lobe)
2. Broncho Pnemonia (lobular, patchy involvement)

Lobar Pnemonia
- 95% strep pneumo (rusty sputum)
- alcoholic : klebsiella infection

Stages
- consolidation (inside alveolar spaces, acute hyperemia, edema)
- Red hepatization (neutrophils and hemorrhage)
- grey hepatization (degradation of rbc)
- resolution healing,

Complications of Pnemonia
- pleural scarring, fibrosis, pleural adhesions
- lung abscess
- empyema
- sepsis
