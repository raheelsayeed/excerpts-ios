Atelectasis
- collapsed lung or not dilating lung
1. Obstructive / Resorption
- Upper airway Obs, lung cannot enter and air within the alveoli is resorption (used up)

- shifts mediastinum toward the collapsed lung

2. Compressive: fluid, air, blood tumor in pleural space. .Lung compliance.
3. Contraction atelectasis: fibrosis and scarring
4. Patchy atelectasis.

Complications: infections (.mucociliary secretions)
Reversible
