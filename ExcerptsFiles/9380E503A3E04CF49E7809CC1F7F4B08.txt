Ketone Synthesis in Starvation

Continued from Beta Oxidation

Ketone Synthesis
-----------
- Mito Matrix in Liver
- Only in Starvation state
- No hormone control

AcetylCoA enters the liver cells and can go two ways from here
1. Krebs or Gluconeo
2. Ketone syn

1. AcetylCoA -- HMGCOA
2. AcetylCoa ---(h gcoa-synthase)-- hMGCoA
2. HMG-CoA  ---{HmgcoaLyase)-- Acetoacetate
4. Acetoacetate + NAD --- 3HydroxyButyrate (BHBA) + NADH
5. HMG-COA ---> Acetone

SIGNALS:
- STARVATION STATE
- In liver, starvation promotes gluconeogenesos, in which OAA is diverted. Since its not a available for krebs, AcetylCoa is diverted to ketone synthesis

Reminder: starvation
- dec insulin
- Inc epi
- inc cortisol
- inc glucagon


The three Ketones get into the blood

ExtraHepatic Sites
1. muscles
2. Renal cortex
3. Brain (prolonged fast)

Reverse reactions to produce AcetylCoA by enzyme Thiophorase and in Other cells, OAA is available for krebs and generate energy via ATP. (No gluconeogenesis)

Thiophorase activates acetoacetate --> AcetoAcetylCoA ---> 2 AcetylCoa


Clinical:
- ketonemia (ketoacidosis) lower pH
- ketonuria (Dehydration as ketones are osmotic and draw water)
- Acetone breath fruity odour
