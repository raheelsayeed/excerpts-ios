Filtered Materials
- Freely filtered TP/P = 1.0

TP - Tubular fluid conc of substance
P - Plasma conc of substance

= 1.0
1. Major electrolytes
2. Metabolic wastes urea, uric acid, creatinine
3. Metabolites: Glu, AA, ketone bodies
4. Non natural substances: inulin, p-aminohippuric acid.
5. Lower weight proteinsand peptides: insulin, myoglobin

> 1.0 - impossible, implies secretion, which does not happen


Not freely filtered ( < 1.0)
1. Album, globumin, plasma proteins
2. Lipid soluble hormones bound to proteins, like T4

Cortisol free is bit bound to proteins and is thus freely filtered, 
Also testosterone, sufficient
