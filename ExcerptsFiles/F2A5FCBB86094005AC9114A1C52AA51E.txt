ACTIVE TRANSPORT
1 PRIMARY AT: TRANSPORTING PORTEIN DIRECTLY uses ATP. Na,K,ATPase, and Calcium dep-ATPase.

2. SECONDARY AT: TRANSPORTING PROTEIN DOES NOT HAVE ATPase activity, indirectly uses ATP. for energy eg. Na-Glu in PT  with N,K,ATPase.
The rate of Absorption of Glu in (PT) depends on the rate Na Gradient across the membrane.

 the other e.g.: Na,H (Antiport), with Na,K,ATPase pump.
Rate of pumping of hydrogen, is based upon the sodium gradient across that membrane.
