Type 1. Familial LIPOPROTEINEMIA

- genetic defect in LPL or ApoC-II.
- high TG
- high Chylomicron & VLDL
- RED ORANGE ERUPTIVE XANTOMAS

- acute pancreatitis
- ABD PAIN GMAFTER FATTY MEAL.
