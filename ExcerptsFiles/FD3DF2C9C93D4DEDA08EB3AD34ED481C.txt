SARCOIDOSIS
- [Etio] Granulomatous, Female>Male,  African Americam woman.
$Symptoms
- Cough
- SOB
- Fatigue
- Malaise
- Skin Lesions
- Eye irritation
- Fever
- Night sweats
Path:
- Diffuse scattered Non caseating granulomas
- Sin, Liver, Spleen, Heart, CNS GI 
- Eye: Mikulicz syndrome: (Uvea and parotid)
- Lab: ^ACE
- Xray: Angle-Wing Appearance: Bilateral hilar / mediastinal adenopathy

Micro:
- Noncaseating granulomas
- schaumann bodies: laminated dystrophic calcifications
- asteroid bodies: stellate giant cell cytoplasmic inclusions

Diagnosis of EXCLUSION: Rule out other causes of Noncaseating granulomas, ACE levels, Hypercalcemia
