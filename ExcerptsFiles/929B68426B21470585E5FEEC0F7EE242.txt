MalAbsorption Syndrome
- SYM: Diarrhea, steatorrea
1. Celiac Sprue (Non tropical sprue)
Hypersensitivity to Gluten and gliadin, resulting in loss of small bowel villi and malabsorption.
High fibre diet: Glutin-in fibre
  - Loss of Villi
  - ↑IEL & ↑PlasmaCells -lamina
  - IgA producing, (Associated w/ IgA Nephropathy, Dermatitis-herpetiformis
  - Genetic: HLA-B8, DR3, DQ
Rx: Avoid Gluten stuff


2. Tropical Sprue
- unknown aetiology
- malabsorption
- h/o Carribean Travelling
- Morphologically: like Celiac - Atrophy.
- [LM] Like Celiac Sprue
- [Rx] Ab, b12, Folate

3. Whipple disease
- infection by Tropheryma Whipplei
- Sym: Malabsoprtion, wtloss, diarrhoea, 
- Lamina Propria: macrophages stuffed with PAS stain, G+ Rods
