- ACAT: Esterification (Storage)

↑Chol: ↓HMG CoA Reductase, ↑ACAT, ↓LDL-Receptor-Gene Expression

- No functional LDL Receptors, 
- ↑LDL-Serum: Hypercholesterolemia
