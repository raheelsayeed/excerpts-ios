LOOP DIURETICS
ACTS ON: TAL-LoH
NA,K, DICHLORIDE SYMPORT (LUMINAL MEMBRANE)
NORMALLY: K LEAKS OUT TO MAINTAIN CONC GRADIENT + POTENTIAL
BECAUSE OF NET+POTENTIAL, Mg, Ca are shunted into interstitial (repelled) because of this luminal net+potential.

BLOCKING THE PUMP: NO NET+POTENTIAL
uses: 
- acute pul edema
- hypercalcemia
- acute renal failure
- anion overdose (Lactate, Ketone)
- heart failure
- REFRACTORY EDEMA
- HYPERTENSION: VASODILATOR: >PROSTAGLANDINS(INC).

SIDENOTE: NSAIDS BLOCKS PRODUTION OF PG. CANCELS ANTI-HTN EFFECT OF LOOP-DIURETICS
