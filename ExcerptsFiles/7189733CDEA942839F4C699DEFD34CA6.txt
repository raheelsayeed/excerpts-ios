Indirect Muscurinic Drugs
AChE Inhibitors: ↑M & ↑N action
1. Endrophonium
2. Physostigmine
3. Neostigmine
4. Pyridostigmine
5. Donepezil (BBB+, Alzheimer's)
6. Organophosphates (BBB+, Irreversible) [anti: Atropin +2-PAN]

[v] Main uses of AChE Inhibition is for Diagnosis or Treatment of diseases that BLOCK "M" or "N" Receptors.
↓AChE = ↑ACh-Action would remove those blockers competitively.
Diseases like
- Myasthenia Gravis
- Cholinergic Crisis (↑Desensitization of N receptors): Muscle paralysis.
- Paralytic ileus
- Neurogenic Bladder (Urinary Retention)
- Reversal of Depolarized Nm Blockers.
