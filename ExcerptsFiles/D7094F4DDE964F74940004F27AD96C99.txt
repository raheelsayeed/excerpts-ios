Membranoproliferative Glomerulonephritis
- NEPHROTIC OR NEPHRITIC
- Type I: from Good-pasture---> nephritic
- II: Dense deposit---- Nephrotic
TYPICALLY SECONDARY TO INFECTION, SLE, ENDOCARD, HBV, HCV, HIV CLL
- [LM] Lobulated glomeruli, mesangial prolix and cap wall thickening (endo,mesangial cell prolif or sub endothelial deposition. 
- - - SPLITTING OF BASEMENT MEMBRANE ("TRAM-TRACKING")
- [IMF] type1-granular C3, IgG, C1q, C4 typ2- granular,linear C3.
- [EM] type1- sub endothelial & mesangial immune complex deposits
 type II dense deposits within GBM.

- [LAB] !SERUMC3, c3-nephritic factor
- typeI: tram-tracking
- typeII dense deposits
