Coronary blood supply
1- left anterior descending coronaries supply: (commonly thrombosed)
  Anterior surface of the heart including the anterior 2/3rd of inter ventricular septum. Bundle fibres are located within the anterior 2/3rd of IVSeptum. 
Complete heart block here will require pace maker. The above artery is the likely site of blockage.

2- right coronary artery thrombosis 
Supplies entire posterior part of the heart
Posterior 1/3rd of inter ventricular septum
Entire right ventricle
Av node 90% , sinus bradycardia
PosteroMedial papillary muscle of mitral valve. Mitral regurgitation!
Sometimes: Epigastric pain. Atypical chest pain, simulates GERD
Sa node has equal distribution between left and right
