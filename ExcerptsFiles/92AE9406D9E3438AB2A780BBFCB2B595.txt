Insulin Transport Liver and Pancreas
Glut2 channel
Beta cell sensor in pancreas sense extra glu in cell and release insulin if higher.

Glu in liver used for biosynthesis of 
- Glycogen
- Fatty acids
- Cholesterol

Lipoprotein Lipase converts TG into Fatty acids which is taken up by adipocytes.
TG cannot go through membrane

Km is 15 mM very high!
